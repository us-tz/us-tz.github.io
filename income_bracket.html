Income Bracket Criteria

Your income bracket is determined by your family's monthly income. Honesty is expected, and any dishonesty will result in immediate termination of service.

Lower Income Bracket (Braveheart)

  • Criteria: Monthly income below TZS 300,000

Lower Middle Income Bracket (Mavericks)

  • Criteria: Monthly income between TZS 300,000 and TZS 1,000,000

Upper Middle Income Bracket (Starlight)

  • Criteria: Monthly income between TZS 1,000,000 and TZS 5,000,000

Higher Income Bracket (Vanguards)

  • Criteria: Monthly income over TZS 5,000,000

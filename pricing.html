Service Pricing and Financial Aid Criteria

The service pricing is dependent on your family's monthly income. Your honesty is expected, and any dishonesty will result in termination of service without reimbursement.

Lower Income Bracket (braveheart)

  • Standard Service Package: $500
  • Financial Aid Eligibility: Full or partial subsidy based on income (e.g., below TZS 300,000 monthly)

Lower Middle Income Bracket (mavericks)

  • Standard Service Package: $500
  • Financial Aid Eligibility: Partial subsidy based on income (e.g., between TZS 300,000 and TZS 1,000,000 monthly)

Upper Middle Income Bracket (starlight)

  • Standard Service Package: $500
  • Financial Aid Eligibility: Limited or no subsidy, based on discretion or special circumstances (e.g., between TZS 1,000,000 and TZS 5,000,000 monthly)

Higher Income Bracket (vanguards)

  • Standard Service Package: $800
  • Financial Aid Eligibility: No subsidy, full fee applies (e.g., over TZS 5,000,000 monthly)
